About James Fedos

James Fedos is an American thriller author, historical novelist, military veteran, actor, editor, and strategic ghostwriter. His work explores leadership, consequence, institutional power, and the people forced to make impossible decisions when systems begin to fail.

Drawing from decades of experience supporting military and federal organizations, James combines operational realism with cinematic storytelling to create novels grounded in authentic decision-making and historical research.

His published works include No Safe Landing, Abort Condition, and The City That Couldn't Sleep. Additional historical and political thrillers are currently in development.
