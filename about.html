About James Fedos

James Fedos is an American thriller and historical fiction author, military veteran, editor, and strategic writer. His work explores power, leadership, consequence, and the individuals who shape history through difficult decisions.

Drawing on years of experience supporting military and government organizations, James brings institutional realism to contemporary thrillers while applying meticulous historical research to his historical fiction.

His published novels include No Safe Landing, Abort Condition, and The City That Couldn't Sleep, with numerous additional novels currently in development.
